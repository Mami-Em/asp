About us

Our mission is to work with local partners here in Madagascar to conserve endangered species and their habitats.

Tony King, Conservation and Reintroduction Coordinator

We have been remarkably successful in averting the extention crisis that was facing the Greater Bamboo Lemur less than ten years ago. Our challenge now is to continue ur support of local communities protecting bamboo Lemurs to ensurethis vival continues, and to actto save even more of one hundred or so other lemur species unique to Madagascar.
